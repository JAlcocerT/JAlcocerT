title: "Cost of living data needs personal constraints"
date: 2026-06-02
tags: ["travel-planning", "cost-of-living", "data-products"]
description: "Cost data is only actionable when it is filtered through personal constraints."
---

Cost of living data is not a travel answer by itself.

The same city can be cheap or expensive depending on rent duration, neighborhood, transport style, health needs, food habits, remote-work setup, and tolerance for inconvenience.

A useful trip planner should ask for constraints before ranking places. Otherwise it compares generic averages instead of comparing realistic personal scenarios.
