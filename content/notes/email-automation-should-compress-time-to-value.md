---
title: "Email automation should compress time to value"
date: 2026-01-21
tags: ["email", "saas", "conversion"]
description: "Email automation is useful when it helps users reach the product value sooner."
---

Email automation should not exist just to send more messages.

Its purpose is to reduce time to value: clarify the next step, remove uncertainty, recover stalled users, answer predictable objections, and help the buyer complete the job they came for.

Automation is conversion infrastructure when it improves momentum.
