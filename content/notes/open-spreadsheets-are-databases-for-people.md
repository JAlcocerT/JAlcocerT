Spreadsheet-like tools succeed because they make structured data feel editable.

A business user often needs forms, tables, formulas, permissions, and automations before they need a custom app. Tools like open spreadsheets can bridge the gap between chaotic sheets and rigid databases.

The value is human-editable structure.
