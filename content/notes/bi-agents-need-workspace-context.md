---
title: "BI agents need workspace context"
date: 2026-02-19
tags: ["bi", "agents", "power-bi"]
description: "BI agents are useful only when they understand datasets, permissions, reports, and business definitions."
---

An agent inside a BI workspace needs more than chart-generation ability.

It needs context about datasets, measures, refreshes, permissions, report intent, semantic models, lineage, and business definitions. Otherwise it becomes a conversational wrapper around incomplete metadata.

BI agents are useful when they respect the workspace as a governed environment.
