title: "Seasonal services need seasonal distribution"
date: 2026-04-18
tags: ["services", "weddings", "marketing"]
description: "A seasonal product must align acquisition, urgency, and delivery with the customer's calendar."
---

Seasonal services have a timing problem before they have a product problem.

For weddings and similar events, demand, urgency, search behavior, and decision windows follow a calendar. Distribution has to meet the buyer when the job becomes urgent.

The service must be ready before the season peaks.
