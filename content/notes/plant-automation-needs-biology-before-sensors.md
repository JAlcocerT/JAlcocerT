---
title: "Plant automation needs biology before sensors"
date: 2026-04-11
tags: ["iot", "plants", "automation"]
description: "Plant IoT systems should measure variables that matter biologically, not only variables that are easy to sense."
---

Plant automation should start from the plant's needs.

Temperature, humidity, soil moisture, light, vapor pressure deficit, and watering schedule matter because they affect growth. Sensors are useful only when they connect to a biological decision.

The system should automate care, not merely collect readings.
