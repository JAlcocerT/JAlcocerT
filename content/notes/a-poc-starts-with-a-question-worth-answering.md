---
title: "A PoC starts with a question worth answering"
date: 2026-04-20
tags: ["poc", "product", "experiments"]
description: "A proof of concept is useful when it resolves a specific uncertainty."
---

A PoC should answer a question.

It might test feasibility, demand, integration risk, conversion, cost, performance, or user behavior. If the PoC is not tied to an uncertainty, it becomes a small product with unclear standards.

The proof comes from what is learned, not what is built.
