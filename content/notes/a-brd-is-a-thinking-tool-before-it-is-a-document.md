---
title: "A BRD is a thinking tool before it is a document"
date: 2026-01-14
tags: ["brd", "product", "requirements"]
description: "A BRD is useful when it clarifies the business frame before implementation starts."
---

A BRD should force the business frame into the open.

Before a prototype, prompt, or codebase exists, the team should know who the user is, what pain is being solved, what value is expected, which constraints matter, and what would make the project a bad idea.

The document matters less than the decisions it exposes.
