A web audit becomes a product when the diagnostic process is repeatable.

Performance, links, metadata, sitemap, robots, accessibility, content, and conversion checks can become a standardized pipeline. The service value comes from interpreting those findings and proposing action.

Productization starts with a repeatable diagnostic.
