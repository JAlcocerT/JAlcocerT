---
title: "Landing pages should sell the next action"
date: 2026-01-02
tags: ["landing-pages", "lead-generation", "product"]
description: "A landing page works when it makes one next action obvious and low-friction."
---

A landing page should not explain everything a product could become.

Its job is to make one next action obvious: request an audit, join a list, book a call, download a resource, or try a demo. The copy, form, analytics, and follow-up flow should all support that action.

This makes landing pages operational tools, not just front pages.
