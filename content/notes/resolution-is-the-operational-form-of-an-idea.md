---
title: "Resolution is the operational form of an idea"
date: 2026-03-30
tags: ["execution", "devops", "product"]
description: "An idea becomes real when it is resolved into decisions, artifacts, and measurable delivery."
---

An idea is not ready just because it sounds clear.

It needs to be resolved into artifacts: requirements, architecture decisions, delivery steps, risks, metrics, and feedback loops. Resolution turns ambition into something a team or agent can execute.

Execution quality depends on how well the idea has been resolved.
