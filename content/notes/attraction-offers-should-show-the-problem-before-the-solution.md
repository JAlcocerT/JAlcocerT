---
title: "Attraction offers should show the problem before the solution"
date: 2026-05-10
tags: ["lead-generation", "offers", "web-audits"]
description: "A scalable service offer works better when prospects see the problem before they evaluate the service."
---

Attraction offers should make the problem visible first.

A free audit, diagnostic, benchmark, or interactive report helps a prospect see the gap before being asked to buy a service. This lowers explanation cost and makes outreach less abstract.

The offer starts with recognition, not persuasion.
