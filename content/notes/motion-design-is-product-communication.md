---
title: "Motion design is product communication"
date: 2026-04-19
tags: ["motion-design", "branding", "ai"]
description: "Motion design helps when it explains state, sequence, and value instead of decorating the interface."
---

Motion design should clarify what the product does.

Animations can reveal sequence, causality, transformation, progress, and before-after value. When they only decorate, they add cost without improving understanding.

Good motion design is communication design with time as a medium.
