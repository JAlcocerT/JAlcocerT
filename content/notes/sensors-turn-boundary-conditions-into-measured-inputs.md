Sensors are a practical way to improve physical models.

Instead of guessing every boundary condition, a system can measure temperature, humidity, irradiance, surface temperature, or other local variables. Those measurements turn an abstract simulation into a calibrated model of a real place.

For thermal and energy projects, IoT is not just monitoring. It is model grounding.
