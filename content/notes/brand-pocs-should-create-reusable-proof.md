---
title: "Brand PoCs should create reusable proof"
date: 2026-05-01
tags: ["brand", "poc", "product"]
description: "A brand PoC is useful when it produces reusable evidence of the offer's value."
---

A brand PoC should create proof that can be reused.

The artifact might be a landing page, case study, audit, demo, video, report, or before-after comparison. It should help future buyers understand the offer faster.

The best PoCs are not throwaways. They become sales assets.
