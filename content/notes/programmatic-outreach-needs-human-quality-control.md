---
title: "Programmatic outreach needs human quality control"
date: 2026-05-18
tags: ["outreach", "automation", "sales"]
description: "Automated outreach becomes risky when targeting, personalization, and timing are not reviewed."
---

Programmatic outreach should not remove judgment.

Automation can gather signals, draft messages, schedule steps, and track responses, but the offer, targeting, personalization, and timing still need quality control. Bad automation scales bad judgment.

The point is leverage, not abdication.
