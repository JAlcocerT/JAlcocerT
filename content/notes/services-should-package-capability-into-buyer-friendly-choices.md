Services should translate capability into choices a buyer can understand.

The provider may think in tools, agents, audits, workflows, automations, and infrastructure. The buyer needs outcomes, scope, price logic, delivery expectations, and a next step.

Packaging is the bridge between technical ability and purchase decision.
