title: "Agentic organizations still need accountability"
date: 2026-02-23
tags: ["agents", "dao", "governance"]
description: "Autonomous agents and DAOs do not remove the need for ownership, incentives, and decision rights."
---

Agents and DAOs do not make accountability disappear.

They can automate tasks, proposals, transactions, and coordination, but someone still defines goals, accepts risk, resolves conflict, and pays for failure. Without clear ownership, autonomy becomes diffusion of responsibility.

The future may be more automated, but governance remains the hard part.
