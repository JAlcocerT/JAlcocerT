title: "Travel plant automation needs failure tolerance"
date: 2026-05-05
tags: ["plants", "iot", "automation"]
description: "Automation for unattended plants should be designed around failure modes, not only ideal schedules."
---

Plant automation matters most when nobody is home.

That means the system should handle failure modes: dry reservoirs, stuck pumps, sensor drift, power loss, network loss, leaks, overwatering, and unusual heat. A schedule alone is not enough.

Unattended automation should be boringly fault-tolerant.
