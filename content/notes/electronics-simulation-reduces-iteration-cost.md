Electronics simulation is a way to make mistakes cheaper.

Before wiring hardware or designing a board, simulation can expose voltage levels, timing, transients, component stress, and design assumptions. It does not replace measurement, but it reduces blind iteration.

The best prototype loop combines simulation, build, and measurement.
