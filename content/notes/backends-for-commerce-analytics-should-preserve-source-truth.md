---
title: "Backends for commerce analytics should preserve source truth"
date: 2026-02-03
tags: ["shopify", "analytics", "backend"]
description: "Commerce analytics backends should keep imported business facts traceable to their source systems."
---

Commerce analytics should not turn source systems into untraceable summaries too early.

Orders, products, payments, campaigns, refunds, and customers need stable identifiers and timestamps so generated insights can be checked against the original truth. Without traceability, AI summaries become difficult to debug.

A good backend protects explainability.
