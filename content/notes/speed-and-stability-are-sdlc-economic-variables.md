---
title: "Speed and stability are SDLC economic variables"
date: 2026-02-13
tags: ["sdlc", "devops", "economics"]
description: "Delivery speed only helps when it does not create rework, outages, or trust loss."
---

Shipping faster is not automatically better.

Speed creates value when the system can absorb change without losing stability. If velocity increases rework, bugs, outages, or customer confusion, the economic benefit disappears.

The SDLC should optimize for learning rate and reliability together.
