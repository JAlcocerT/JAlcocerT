---
title: "Local service marketplaces need trust before matching"
date: 2026-07-01
tags: ["marketplaces", "lead-generation", "services"]
description: "A local service marketplace works only when both sides trust the request, provider, and price signal."
---

Matching demand and supply is not enough for local services.

The customer needs trust in the provider, quote, availability, and outcome. The provider needs trust in the lead quality and job details. Without that, a marketplace becomes noisy lead forwarding.

Trust design comes before scale.
