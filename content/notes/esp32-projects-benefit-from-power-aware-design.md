ESP32 projects should be designed around power from the start.

Sensors, Wi-Fi, sleep modes, battery, solar input, sampling frequency, and enclosure constraints all shape whether the device works outside the desk. 

Firmware and hardware choices are coupled.

Field IoT is power management plus sensing.
