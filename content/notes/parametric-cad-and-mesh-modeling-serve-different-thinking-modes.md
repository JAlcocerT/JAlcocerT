title: "Parametric CAD and mesh modeling serve different thinking modes"
date: 2026-03-20
tags: ["blender", "cad", "design"]
description: "CAD is strongest for intent and constraints; mesh tools are strongest for form and rendering."
---

Parametric CAD and mesh modeling are not interchangeable.

CAD tools preserve design intent through dimensions, constraints, assemblies, and editable features. Mesh tools are better for sculpting, visual composition, animation, and rendering. Confusing the two creates brittle workflows.

A good design pipeline lets each representation do its proper job.
