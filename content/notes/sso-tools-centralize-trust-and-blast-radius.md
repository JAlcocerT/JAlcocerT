SSO tools simplify authentication by centralizing trust.

That makes access easier to manage, but it also concentrates blast radius: misconfiguration, weak recovery, exposed admin panels, or poor MFA can affect every connected service. Identity infrastructure needs extra care.

Convenience and risk move to the same place.
