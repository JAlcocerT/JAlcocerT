title: "Active damping is control theory applied to vibration"
date: 2026-04-29

description: "Active damping works by measuring vibration and injecting a counter-response through a controlled actuator."
---

Active damping turns vibration isolation into a control problem.

Instead of relying only on passive stiffness and damping, the system measures vibration and applies a counter-response. That requires sensors, actuators, latency control, stability margins, and a model of the disturbance.

The mechanical problem becomes a signal-processing loop.
