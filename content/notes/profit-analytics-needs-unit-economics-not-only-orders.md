description: "E-commerce analytics becomes useful when it connects orders to gross profit, CAC, and operating costs."
---

Revenue analytics can hide whether an e-commerce business is healthy.

A useful analytics layer connects orders to cost of goods, refunds, shipping, ad spend, discounting, payment fees, inventory risk, and customer acquisition cost. Profitability depends on the full unit economics, not only sales volume.

The question is not what sold. It is what was worth selling.
