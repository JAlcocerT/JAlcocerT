RevOps matters when it connects the whole revenue loop.

Market signals become leads, leads become conversations, conversations become pipeline, pipeline becomes revenue or rejection, and rejection improves the next targeting pass. If the loop is broken, teams keep generating activity without learning.

Revenue operations is feedback infrastructure.
